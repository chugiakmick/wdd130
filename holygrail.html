Permaculture Basic Description

Permaculture is basically a philosophy of working with nature rather than trying to impose your own order.
This is done by picking plants and animals that will create a small ecosystem within the larger, local and regional ecosystem.
We do this by filling as many ecological niches as possible with species of our own choosing that will produce products or services we want,
or support the production of products we want.


EXAMPLE: Chickens in a mixed orchard

A very simple example might be putting chickens and geese in a mixed fruit orchard. The fruit trees provide a product (fruit) and shade to protect the ground,
while the chickens provide several products or services such as pest control, automatically applied fertilizer, weed control, cleaning up fallen fruit, eggs and meat.
Of course these things need to be balanced. A single chicken in an huge orchard would not provide enough,while a million chickens around three trees would provide far to much fertilizer
and would become a detrimental force in the orchard.
This is in keeping with the Lords command to Adam to care for the Garden in which he was placed.
